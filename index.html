Scoring: Every time you break a brick, you earn 100 points. Complete all the bricks on the screen to advance to the next level and earn a bonus score!

Levels: Each time you clear a set of bricks, you'll move up a level. The ball speed and player movement speed will increase, making the game more challenging. The game has a maximum of 10 brick rows.

Game Over: If the ball touches the bottom of the screen, it's game over. Press Space to restart the game from the beginning.

Good luck, and aim for the highest score!
